Illustrator Assignment

P5.js sketch



Title: Relaxing Rounds
Semester/Year:2020
Coding/programming languages: p5.js
Description: The General concept is calmness and simplicity. I wanted to have something the user could interact with simply and have both a big and small effect on. It's inspired by a project I did previously, I chose circles and squares because their shape allows for a nice looking arrangement. The user can interact with the program in two ways, Depending on the position of the mouse the user will alter the color of the two patterns. AE Animation

Title: Contentment
Semester/Year: 2020
Software: Photoshop (and Fire Alpaca)
Description: I decided to animate "contentment" because it's an emotion that most people seek to feel. It's also an emotion I really enjoy feeling. I've found that I can be content by just enjoying someone's presence for a while, ususally while enjoying a nice view and maybe while having a nice relaxed discussion, so that's what i decided to animate. I found the colors to be reminescent of a cityscape and sky during twilight, so those are the colors I decided to use throughout the piece. The most challenging part of the project was being unable to use After Effects. It just refused to work well with my computer so I had to give up on using it, and opt for photoshop and a drawing program instead. I think the piece came out pretty well, though I do wish I could incoporate sound into it.
